## Cleaning the Espresso Machine
  1. Soak portafilters in detergent and hot water
  2. Brush group head gaskets with hot water for 10 seconds
  3. Backflush group heads first with detergent, then just water (10 second cycles)
  4. Remove dispersion screens and scrub with brush and hot water and replace
  5. Rinse and scrub portafilters and baskets clean
  6. Soak steam wands in hot water
  7. Remove, clean and replace all drip trays
  8. Pour hot water down milk pitcher rinser
  9. Setup station including towels and pitchers for the next crew. 

## Emergency Procedures

For Violence/Physical Harm or Threats: 
  Ask the guest/employee to leave. Ask for backup from another staff member if needed. 
    If the guest/employee refuses to leave, call 911. 
  Call the General Manager (205.842.2918) and report the event. 

For Intoxication/Unwanted Rowdiness: 
  Do not serve beer/liquor to anyone who appears intoxicated. 
  Verbally inform the guest that they are cut off from alcohol and offer food/water. 
  If they continue to be a problem, ask them to leave. If they refuse, refer to VIOLENCE/PHYSICAL HARM above. 
  Call EMS (911) if a guest is unresponsive. 
  If the situation requires additional resolution, call the General Manager (205.842.2918). If the GM is not needed, send a DM in Slack outlining the situation and what steps were taken. 

For Fires: 
  Call 911. 
  Call the General Manager (205.842.2918). 

For Leaks: - Mouse Hole 
  Immediately turn off water to the source of the leak. (*Note: The main water line into the building is in the corner near the projector screen.)
  Attempt to reaffix or tighten the source of the leak. 
  If water is necessary for continued service, call the General Manager (205.842.2918). 
  If the water source can be bypassed for operations, send the General Manager a DM in Slack. 
  Follow any further instructions provided by the General Manager. 
  If the GM cannot be reached, in case of an emergency, reach out to Grady Wright (210.316.5572). 

For Localized (Single-Outlet) Power Outages: 
  Check the GFI indicator on the plug. If it is tripped, reset it. 
  Check the breakers. If a breaker is tripped, reset the breaker. 
  If the outage causes normal operations to be impossible, call the General Manager (205.842.2918). 
  If the outage can be bypassed for operations, send the General Manager a DM on Slack. 

For No-Shows/No Coverage: 
  Assess whether the shift can be run with those present. 
  If additional coverage is needed, call the General Manager (205.842.2918). 
  If you can run without additional coverage, send the GM a DM. 

For Out of Stock Items: 
  Offer an alternative to the guest. 
  Check for backups. 
  Post a message in the #86ed Channel on Slack noting what is out of stock. 

For Homeless Folks: 
  If water is requested, we can provide water in to-go cups. No other items should be given out for free. 
  Under no circumstances is anyone permitted to beg/busk on the property. 
  If further action is needed, follow the protocol for Violence/Physical Harm or Threats above. 
  In the presence of the police officer, inform the individual that they are not allow on the premises again and are barred from returning. 

For Theft: 
  If robbed during business hours, comply with demands made by the robber. 
  Immediately call the police (911) and the General Manager (205.842.2918). 
  For robberies that occur after hours, call the General Manager. 

O2 Sensor: 
  If the O2 Sensor is going off, press and hold the button all the way on the right to clear the alert. 
  Send a message in #beerwinecider in Slack noting the issue. 

Foamy Beer Lines: 
  Uncouple and disconnect the offending line in the walkin. 
  Reconnect the draft line and try again. 
  If the problem persists, send a message in #beerwinecider in Slack noting the issue. 

MOD Bar Break: 
  Assess whether you can operate. 
  If you are able to operate (even without one group head), send a DM to the General Manager in Slack. 
  If you are unable to operate, call the General Manager (205.842.2918). 

Internet Outages: 
  Don’t change the wifi connection for Toast. Doing so may delete any captured payments. 
  Reset the router by turning the powerstrip off in the power alley and waiting 2 minutes. 
  Check to see if resetting the router has corrected the issue. 
  If it hasn’t, the issue may reside with the landlord. 
  Call the General Manager to pursue further corrections with the landlord. 
  Online orders need to be turned off when the wifi is down. Ping KM or GM to get this accomplished.
