#Back of House (BOH) Kitchen Frequently Asked Questions#

Q. Are our bagels Vegan?
Yes.
Q. Are our hashbrowns Gluten free?
Yes.
Q. Is our grits Gluten Free?
Yes, but not certified.
Q. Is our granola Gluten Free?
Yes, but not certified.
Q. Does our granola contain nuts? What kind?
Yes. Assume all kinds. We get granola from a third party and assume there is a level of wiggle in ingredient list.
Q. What if customer has a sesame allergy?
Let them know we do not recommend eating at Brew and Brew.
Q. Does our Salsa Macha have nuts?
No, but it does have seeds. Sesame and Pumpkin seeds 
Q. Does the Beyond sausage have nuts?
No, but they do contain pea protein. Peas are legumes and, although not common, can effect people with peanut and legume allergies.
Q. Does Beyond Sausage contain Soy?
No .
Q. How much should I charge to swap out carbohydrate or bread?
When in doubt ring in an existing sandwich which contains that carbohydrate and manually notate what type of build. The carbohydrate in most instances dictates the cost. Example: Customer wants SOB on Croissant. Ring in Croissant Sandwich. Notate SOB build.
Q. Can I swap swiss for American cheese and vice versa?
Sure! Just notate.

Q. I didn't receive bagel delivery what do I do?
Ask a MOD to call Rosen's Brick and mortar on Burnett - (512) 487-5027 - to see if they are on their way. Make sure to edit bagel counts. If they are not arriving for some reason have MOD to run to whole foods.
Q. I was shorted an item on this delivery, what do I do?
Let delivery driver know and have it notated on both copies of invoice. Check to see if that item is 911 or not and let MOD know when you might need it. Slack DM Kitchen manager and let them know what was shorted. Make sure to tell km if it was notated on invoice or not!
