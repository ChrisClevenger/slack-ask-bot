Cleaning the Espresso Machine / Modbar / Mod Bar

1. Soak portafilters in Cafiza and hot water
2. Brush group head gaskets with hot water for 10 seconds
3. Backflush group heads first with detergent, then just water (10 second cycles)
4. Remove dispersion screens and scrub with brush and hot water and replace
5. Rinse and scrub portafilters and baskets clean
6. Soak steam wands in hot water
7. Remove, clean and replace all drip trays
8. Pour hot water down milk pitcher rinser
9. Setup station including towels and pitchers for the next crew.
