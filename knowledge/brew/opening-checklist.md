Brew & Brew Opening Checklist 

1. Clock in. 
2. Check Slack for new updates. 
3. Set out stools, trash cans, bush bins. 
4. Clean any debris off of patio and wipe tables. 
5. Empty dishwasher filter and turn on.
6. Turn on frozen machine. 
7. Fill front ice bin. 
8. Fill customer watering tank. 
9. Brew two pots of coffee.
10. Wipe down and set out fresh menus.
11. Setup condiment shelf.
12. Remove tap plugs and soak.
13. Dial in espresso.
14. Unlock the hallway door.
15. Stock bathroom.
16. Scrub toilets with a little bleach. 
17. Wipe tables and level with wedges.
18. Stock both lowboys.
19. Setup pastry display.
20. Rinse drains with hot water.
21. Turn on appropriate music. 
22. Put something on the projector. 
23. Unlock both front doors.
24. Turn on neon signs.
