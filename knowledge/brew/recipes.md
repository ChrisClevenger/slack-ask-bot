# Front of House Coffee and Syrup Recipes 

category: coffee
audience: staff
topics: coffee recipes, syrups, sauces, drinks 

## Espresso Coffee Syrup Recipes 

### Vanilla Syrup Recipe

3 tablespoons vanilla bean paste
2 quarts granulated sugar
3-4 quarts hot water (fill the cambro)  
Combine, label, date, and put in fridge 

### Honey Syrup (Honey Bunny Recipe)

#### Honey Syrup

45 fluid ounces (64 oz by weight) Fain's Honey
2 Quarts Hot Water 
Stir to incorporate 
Label, date, and put in fridge

#### Cayenne Cacao Powder 

4 Tablespoons Cacao Powder
12 Dashes Ground Cayenne Pepper 
Combine, label, and put in shaker

#### Orange Mist

5 grams orange essential oil
20 grams filtered water
20 grams any high proof clear liquor 
Combine, put in spray bottle, label, and date 

### Lavender Syrup Recipe 

2 quarts granulated sugar 
12 grams lavender 
3-4 quarts hot water (fill the cambro)
Steep lavender in water/sugar syrup for 20 minutes
Strain, label, date, and put in fridge 

### Nice Spice Syrup Recipe 

2 cup Turbinado Sugar (430g)
6 cups water
2oz/59g Vanilla
3/4 tsp salt
Whisk in 40g Speculoos spice
Continue to whisk until incorporated
Bring to Boil
Simmer until Thickened (about 1.5 hours)

### Booberry Syrup Recipe 

32oz bag 907g frozen blueberries 
10 cups water (less if not using frozen)
907g (1 bag) Turbinado Sugar
4oz/118g Vanilla Extract/Paste
2 tsp Salt
10 Cinnamon Sticks
1 TBSP Cinnamon Powder
Bring to Boil and Simmer for 1/2 Hour
Remove Cinnamon Sticks and Blend
Return to  Simmer till Thickened (about 1.5 hours)

### Strawberry Feels 

6 10z bags Organic Strawberries
3 Cups Water
32oz/907 grams Turbinado Sugar
4oz/118 grams Vanilla
3/4tsp Salt
Simmer Until Thickened
Milk Blend: 1 Box Coconut Milk, Fill to 2 Quarts with Oat Milk

### Zesty Zombie 

Combine:
Handful Star Anise
3 Cups Pomegranate Juice
1 Cup Turbinado Sugar
Bring To Boil, Simmer 20 Minutes

Combine the following:

600 grams Pomegranate Anise Syrup
1000 grams Evergreen Chai
100 grams Lemon Juice
Yields - 2 Quarts

### Live Lav Lime 

Combine:
1500 grams Honey Simple
6 TBSP Lavender Buds
Bring To Boil
Turn off and Simmer 25 Minutes

Combine the following:

1200 grams Lavender Honey Syrup
875 grams Lime Juice
Yields - ~2 quarts
