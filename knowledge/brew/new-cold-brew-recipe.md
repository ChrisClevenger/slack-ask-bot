#New Cold Brew Recipe#

Use Power Button from Super Thing Coffee Roasters
Coffee Input: 1250 grams
Total Water Input: 5 Gallons
EK43 Coffee Grind Setting: 5 
Kieselsol - 1/4 Teaspoon (1.2 grams)
Brew Time: 10 Hours
Yield: 4 Gallons

Put Brewer Shelf in bottom of Toddy Bucket
Place Filter Bag in Toddy Bucket
Put Toddy Bucket under EK43 Spout
Grind 1250 grams coffee on #5 grind (grind directly into Filter Bag)
Add 2 gallons water directly onto the grounds
Stir with Bar Spoon to incorporate
Use Ziptie to tie off Filter Bag (closer to top that to the grounds)
Add 3 gallons of Filtered Water
Add 1/4 Teaspoon or 1.2 grams of Kieselsol
Steep 10 hours
Drain into Keg or pitchers/vessels
(do not tip or slosh Toddy Bucket)
* The Filter Bag will retain some product, drain entirely, lift/squeeze to assist
* Drain only down to the spout then discard the remaining cold brew & sludge
